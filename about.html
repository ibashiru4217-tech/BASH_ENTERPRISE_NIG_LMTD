Our Story

From one workshop to a name riders recognise

Bash Enterprise was founded in 2026 in Anguwan Mall Yaro, along Grave Yard Road in Kofa Toto LGA, Nasarawa State, with a simple goal: give motorcycle owners a place they could trust for honest deals and quality work.

What started as a small motorcycle buying and repair point has grown into a full-service business — covering sales, repairs, maintenance, engine overhauling and genuine spare parts — all without losing the personal, community-first way we treat every customer.

Today, Bash Enterprise is known locally for straight talk, fair pricing, and technicians who take the time to get the job done right the first time.
